Lasagna

Home

Description

A great dish to make for friends and family! Try our recipe!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses

How to Assemble

  1. Cook the meat with garlic and onion
  2. Stir in tomatoes sauce ans water. Season with sugar and the spices and seasonings you prefer
  3. Cook the lasagna noodles in boiling water with salt
  4. Preheat the oven at 190 degrees celsius
  5. Assemble the lasagna by creating layers of sauce, noodles, sauce and cheese (in this order)
  6. Bake for 25 minutes
